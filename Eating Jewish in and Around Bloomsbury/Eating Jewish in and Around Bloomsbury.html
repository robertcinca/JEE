Eating Jewish in and Around Bloomsbury


Nu, really?
First and foremost: there’s something about Jews and fish.  The mecca of Jews and fish remains Foreman & Son of Fish Island, but there are other notable places.
http://www.formans.co.uk/index.php

North Sea
North Sea, on Leigh Street in Bloomsbury, just off of Marchmont Street, has Jewish-style cod roe, and great fish generally
http://www.northseafishrestaurant.co.uk

Gaby’s Deli
Great hot salt beef and falafel in the same place:  Gaby’s.   OK, a bit out of Bloomsbury but worth the shlep down Tottenham Court Road, then Charing Cross Road
http://www.timeout.com/london/restaurants/gabys
