Jewish Daily Post


Back at Whitechapel High Street, a little to the left above the shop ‘Albert’s’ you’ll see a beautiful emblem  with a Star of David.  That was to mark the offices of the Jewish Daily Post (some say ‘News’), one of many Jewish newspapers (back in the days when people read newspapers).  It was made by Arthur Szyk (1894-1951), an interesting artist.  He didn’t like the anarchists.  He himself was a rather right-wing Zionist.  But he was a pretty good artist and excellent craftsman.   The biography by Joseph Ansell, Arthur Szyk:  Artist, Jew, Pole (2004) is good but perhaps a bit generous.
