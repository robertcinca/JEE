Ping Pong and Jewish Culture


Just to the west of Spitalfields Market, on the way to Elder Street:
There is a Ping-Pong table for public use.  Ping-Pong, or table-tennis, used to be one of the favored sports of Jews in London, the UK, and much of the world.

The UCL Department of Hebrew & Jewish Studies is a strict observer of the holiness of Ping-Pong.   Do read Howard Jacobson’s The Mighty Walzer.   It’s much better than The Finkler Question.
