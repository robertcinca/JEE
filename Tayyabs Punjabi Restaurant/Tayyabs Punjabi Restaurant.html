Tayyabs Punjabi Restaurant


Keep walking down Fieldgate Street and you’ll hit Tayyabs (83-89 Fieldgate Street, E1).  It’s not just one of the world’s greatest Punjabi restaurants—it’s one of the best restaurants—period.  You’ll find a wonderful mix of clientele from all backgrounds.

http://www.tayyabs.co.uk

History meets food again.  And religion.  Tayyabs is Halal, so if you want beer or wine you’ll have to bring your own.  They don’t mind.
Order the famous mixed grill.  (It’s not on the menu.)
