Lenin and Bloomsbury


Lenin.  Lenin?  Jewish?  Bloomsbury?
Who knew?  Go figure.

  • Yes, Lenin spent some time in Bloomsbury.
  • You can find a blue plaque on 36 Tavistock Place, just moments from UCL.
  • If interested, the best book is Lenin’s Jewish question by Yohanan Petrovsky-Shtern (New Haven:  Yale University Press, 2010)
