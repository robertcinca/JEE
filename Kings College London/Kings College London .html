In the Lenny Bruce view of "Jewish" versus "Goyish": UCL is Jewish, King's is Goyish.

King’s College London, now an outstanding and (mostly) liberal institution, has a rather unsavory history with regard to Jews.  Maybe not as bad as Oxbridge, but not exactly flattering.  KCL owes its very existence to members of the London’s conservative, Anglican establishment being appalled by the idea that a university in London would actually welcome Jews and other motley types.
But KCL did become home to some important Jewish scholars, like Rosalind Franklin and H. G. Adler.
