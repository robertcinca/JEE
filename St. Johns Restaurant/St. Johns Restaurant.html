St John’s Restaurant


Call me crass. Let’s go from the soup kitchen for the poor—now, of course, turned into expensive flats--to one of the best restaurants in the East End:  St Johns in Spitalfields.   It’s, well, not quite kosher, as you may surmise from its piggy logo.  One of my favorite proper sit-down restaurants in London, it’s famous for making fabulous dishes out of parts of beasts you didn’t even know existed or may have found disgusting.

Link to the restaurant website: https://www.stjohngroup.uk.com/
My favorite foodie writer, Jay Rayner, loves this place (St. Johns, Spitalfields).  I even saw him eating here, really enjoying himself.
