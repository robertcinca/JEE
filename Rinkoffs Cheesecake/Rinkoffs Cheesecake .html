Rinkoffs Cheesecake


Are you hungry for cheesecake?  It’s a shame that there aren’t more remaining Jewish bakeries.  But there is still a fabulous place for cheesecake in the East End, which supplies to a number of other places.
I don't say this lightly:  Rinkoff's challah is the best in the world.
