Sandys Row Synagogue


On the more traditional side:  Sandys Row Synagogue, 4A Sandys Row, London, W1 7HW
This is the front of Sandys Row, which is still in use.  (It’s just moments away from Dirty Dicks pub.)   It’s been fixed up and remains quite simple in design.   I find it elegant and earthy, in the best sense.  In the great English tradition the apostrophe is missing.  (Sandys, as opposed to “Sandy’s Row.”  Thank you to Jann Matlock for reminding me of this quirk.)

Do see if it’s possible to go inside. (Sandys Row).

The remaining East End synagogues, such as Sandys Row, provide some great photo-ops.
